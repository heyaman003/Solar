ESG

Caring for Generations to Come

At Solar, our core mission centres on sustainable development. We are committed to creating enduring value for all our stakeholders through best-in-class, sustainable products. Our holistic ESG strategy, built on four pillars and comprising twelve focus areas, drives us to create a positive impact on the society at large while minimising our environmental footprint.

Towards a
Greener Tomorrow

Our environmental priorities are an integral part of our corporate ethos, guiding our product creation, processes and policies as we endeavour to shape a better future together. Remaining steadfast in our commitment to sustainability, we invest prudently in eco-friendly technologies, lowering resource dependence and reducing our environmental impact. We monitor climate change, strive hard to curb greenhouse gas emissions and employ innovative energy-saving solutions. We collaborate with our value chain partners to implement sustainable solutions throughout our value chain.
